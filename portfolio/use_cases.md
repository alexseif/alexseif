## 🔒 CASE STUDY 02: UAE Portal (Forensic Audit & Infrastructure Hardening)

### 1. Objective & Scope
Brought in to perform a deep forensic code and infrastructure audit on an undocumented, fragmented state-level vehicle registration platform suffering from severe stability risk.

### 2. Technical Decisions & Implementation
* **Operational Risk Identification:** Uncovered that the live production environment was running blindly on a localized WAMP stack with highly restricted operational controls. This environment completely lacked basic mechanisms for scalability, monitoring, disaster recovery, and security hardening.
* **Technical Debt Remediation:** Identified that system knowledge was entirely tribal and documentation was non-existent. The database and application layers had grown organically for years without structural governance, creating massive hidden liabilities for business continuity.
* **Business Outcome:** Translated these findings into clear, objective structural realities for non-technical stakeholders, proving that the real threat to the platform wasn't minor runtime bugs, but the fundamental risks inside the architecture and operational model itself.
