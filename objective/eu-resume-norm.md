# EU Resume Standardization Norms (Tech Sector Calibration)

This document establishes the structural parameters, data layout, and compliance principles required to pass ATS filters and HR screeners within the European Union (with special focus on UK, DACH, and Benelux tech regions).

## 1. Structural Constraints & Layout Design
- **Length Metric:** Maximum 2 pages for standard roles; strict limit of 3 pages for individuals with 20+ years of deep engineering tenures. 
- **Typography Matrix:** Standard, low-noise sans-serif typefaces (Inter, Helvetica, Arial). Completely eliminate decorative styling, graphic bars, or percentage-based skill meters which cause parsing execution failures in modern applicant tracking systems (ATS).
- **Data Density Layout:** Use a clear chronological format. Avoid complex multi-column sidebars for core content; screen readers and parsers read text layouts strictly from left-to-right, top-to-bottom.

## 2. Personal Information Mandates & Privacy Rules
- **GDPR Compliance:** Do not include exact physical addresses, dates of birth, marital status, nationality, or religious affiliations. Including these triggers legal filtering liabilities for EU recruiters and can cause automated application rejection.
- **Required Header Geometry:** - Full Legal Name
  - Professional Designation (e.g., Systems Architect)
  - Contact Channel (Direct Email + Phone with country prefix)
  - Clear Location Vector (e.g., "Cairo, Egypt — Open to international relocation")
  - Verified Digital Citations (Clean LinkedIn URL + Portfolio/GitHub Link)

## 3. The Professional Experience Paradigm
- **Chronological Rule:** Current or most recent technical engagements must occupy the highest vertical position.
- **The Context-Action-Result Metric:** Every bullet point must detail the specific technical problem, the architectural decision executed, and the structural or business performance result. Banish empty marketing buzzwords.
- **Contractor / Freelance Notation:** For multi-year independent consultancy careers, group engagements under a unified professional heading (e.g., "Principal Consultant / Technical Partner") to show continuous, high-autonomy project delivery instead of fragmented, short-term work histories.

## 4. Skills Parsing Schema
- **Decoupled Skill Matrix:** Technologies must be explicitly categorized by layer (Languages, Frameworks, Cloud Infrastructure, Database/Queues) rather than dumped into an amorphous text block.
- **Relevance Targeting:** Only list core technologies where you have active engineering ownership. Outdated ecosystems must be archived into deep background text to prevent irrelevant automated resume matching.
