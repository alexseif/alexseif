## 🗂️ Project Dossier: Aman Portal - Ministry of Interior (2020)
* **Project Name:** Aman Portal (Egyptian Traffic Authority Ecosystem)
* **Timeline:** 2020
* **URL:** http://site.aman-egypt.com/
* **Engagement Type:** Outsourcing Partner via iSoft
* **Architectural Identity:** Software Architect
* **Scale & Jurisdiction:** Deployed at a sovereign state scale for the Egyptian Republic. Engineered directly under the strategic authority of the Egyptian Ministry of Interior (MOI) and the Egyptian Traffic Authority to digitize national public safety and vehicle compliance services.

### 🧠 System Architecture & Micro-Logistics Engine
* **Multichannel Fleet & Facility Orchestration:** Designed and deployed a customized Drupal infrastructure optimized to map, index, and manage automated appointment allocation for a mixed nationwide service fleet. 
* **The Three Sovereign Service Pillars:** The backend cleanly segregates and handles workflow logic, user intakes, and capacity balancing for three distinct governmental divisions:
  1. **Technical Inspection Centers** (مراكز الفحص الفني)
  2. **Driving Training Schools** (مراكز تدريب القيادة)
  3. **Electronic Traffic Units** (وحدات المرور الإلكترونية)
* **Dynamic Geo-Spatial Slot Allocation:** Engineered the core scheduling algorithms and relational database schemas to dynamically calculate real-time availability. The engine accurately tracks changing resource slots across static state facilities, commercial shopping mall kiosks/stalls, and roaming mobile service units, matching citizen location requests with their exact service needs.

### 🚨 National Security & Performance Isolation
* **Sovereign Network Compliance:** Adhered to strict military and state-grade data governance, encryption protocols, and connection parameters required to handle sensitive national vehicle identities and citizen documentation profiles securely.
* **Friction-Free Public Interface:** Optimized backend query execution and map-rendering pipelines so that citizens of all technical fluencies can effortlessly search for, filter, and secure an e-traffic slot without stalling the server during peak daytime operating hours.

* **Technical Stack:** Drupal Enterprise CMS, Advanced Geo-Location Routing APIs, Relational Scheduling Matrices, Sovereign Infrastructure Security, Web Performance Tuning.
* **Engineering Notes:** Contracted via iSoft during a highly successful multi-year government solutions run. This project acts as a major domestic benchmark, showing an ability to take massive, chaotic, real-world physical public assets (mobile trucks, mall stalls) and cleanly capture their operational rules in stable, high-performance web code.
