## 🗂️ Project Dossier: Alexander Restaurant (2025)
* **Project Name:** Alexander Restaurant
* **Timeline:** 2025
* **URL:** https://alexander-restaurant.com/
* **Engagement Type:** Direct Client
* **Architectural Identity:** Technical Partner (Strategic Handholding & Minimalist Execution)
* **Business Context:** A local hospitality business requiring a fast, reliable, and completely friction-free digital menu asset without the unnecessary monthly overhead or technical complexity of premium corporate frameworks.
* **The Engineering Philosophy:** Demonstrates tactical restraint. Rather than over-engineering with heavy plugins or decoupled frameworks, the solution utilized a highly optimized WordPress base theme. This guaranteed near-instantaneous page-load speeds on mobile devices and a highly intuitive content update pipeline for the non-technical client.
* **Technical Stack:** WordPress, Base Theme Customization, Responsive Web Design, Speed Optimization.
* **Engineering Notes:** Executed a minimal-overhead development strategy to deliver a cost-effective, high-performance digital menu that completely eliminates platform complexity for a local hospitality business.
