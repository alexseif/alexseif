# Project Dossier
A minimalist, high-speed digital menu asset for a commercial hospitality client requiring an instantly accessible operational platform. Avoided over-engineering or framework bloat, favoring a hyper-optimized base theme.

### Core Architecture & System Engineering
- **Tactical Restraint:** Rather than leveraging heavy decoupled architectures or subscription-dependent plugins, the solution was built to completely eliminate platform complexity.
- **Speed & Stability:** Guaranteed near-instantaneous page-load speeds on mobile endpoints, removing friction for the end-user while providing a simple content update pipeline for non-technical management.

### Engineering Notes / Edge Cases
Demonstrates the strategic value of restraint. The mandate was to deliver a high-performance digital asset with zero monthly overhead, executing strict minimalist execution as a Technical Partner.
